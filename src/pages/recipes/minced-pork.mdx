<h1 className="text-4xl mb-4 font-heading">Vegan Minced Pork</h1>

<h3 className="text-2xl mb-4">Ingredients</h3>

- 1 12oz package of impossible meat
- 1 tablespoon grated ginger
- 1 tablespoon neutral oil
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 table spoon white wine
- At least 4 scallion stalks
- Some sesame seeds
- Your choice of rice (short grain)

<h3 className="text-2xl my-4">Assembly</h3>

Whisk together wine, soy sauce and sugar

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Once hot, add "meat" and grated ginger. Cook until brown

Stir in the sauce you made earlier, simmer for a few minutes

Add your desired amount of green onions and sesame seeds.

Serve over rice, impress anyone you cook this for
